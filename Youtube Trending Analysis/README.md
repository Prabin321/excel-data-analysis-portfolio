
# 📊 YouTube Trending Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes 6,000+ trending YouTube videos to identify key performance drivers influencing content reach and engagement.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To understand what factors contribute to a video trending on YouTube by analyzing engagement metrics and proposing data-driven recommendations.

## 🔍 Key Analysis
- Analyzed 6,351 trending videos to identify engagement patterns
- Compared performance across categories and video attributes
- Evaluated whether 2M+ views qualify as exceptional based on context
- Identified limitations of basic metrics (views, likes)

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Engagement varies significantly across categories
- Early engagement and content timing influence trending probability
- Views alone are not sufficient to explain performance

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Optimize content timing and publishing strategy
- Improve thumbnails and titles to increase click-through rate (CTR)
- Focus on audience retention and engagement metrics
- Leverage category-specific trends to maximize reach

---

## 🧠 Advanced Analysis
Proposed additional variables for deeper insights:
- Watch Time
- Audience Retention
- Click-Through Rate (CTR)
- Comment Sentiment
- Upload Timing
- Channel Size
